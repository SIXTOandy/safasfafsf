2.Filipino dish Called PARES

Filipino Pares is a popular dish known for its savory and slightly sweet beef stew,
typically served with garlic rice and sometimes a side of broth.
The name pares means "pair" in Filipino, reflecting the traditional pairing of the beef stew with rice.
Here's a basic recipe to help you make this comforting dish:

The Ingredients are:

  • 1 kg beef shank or brisket, cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown s ugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup beef broth or water
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper (or to taste)
  • 1 medium potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 medium carrot, peeled and sliced
